I'm a fourth-year undergraduate student from School of Mechanical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research interests include efficient intelligent robot systems, large language models, and embodied AI. 

I am currently advised by Prof. [Xiao Li](https://me.sjtu.edu.cn/teacher_directory1/lixiao.html). I have served as a reviewer for robotics-related journals and conferences, such as RA-L and ICRA.
